Where Student Voice Meets Smart Innovation

This system is designed to collect and analyze feedback from students participating in a five-day Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning workshop. Students share their learning experiences through open-ended feedback using natural language.

Using Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing techniques, the system automatically classifies feedback into Positive, Neutral, or Negative sentiment. The insights generated help instructors and administrators understand session effectiveness, identify challenges, and improve future training programs.
